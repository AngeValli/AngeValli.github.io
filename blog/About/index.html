About

I am 28 years old. I am French and Spanish. I was born and raised in Port-Saint-Louis-Du-Rhône, near Marseille, France.

PhD Graduate in Applied Mathematics

I conducted my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Abdel Lisser, Université Paris-Saclay and Prof. Sihem Tebbani, CentraleSupélec from Laboratoire des signaux et systèmes, Université Paris-Saclay, CNRS, CentraleSupélec. Prof. Abdel Lisser is also a member of Fédération de Mathématiques de CentraleSupélec, Université Paris-Saclay, CNRS, CentraleSupélec. My research interests lie in probability, optimization, optimal control, optimal transport, machine learning, and financial mathematics.

I am currently a visiting scholar at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, Division of Mathematical Sciences, in the research group of Prof. Ariel Neufeld. I am working with Dr. Dawen Wu on solving high-dimensional systems of ordinary differential equations using physics-informed neural networks.

Curriculum

Before starting my doctoral contract at Université Paris-Saclay, I worked as a quantitative analyst contractor at BNP Paribas CIB, within the Quantitative Research department, focusing on equity derivatives. I hold a master’s degree in Mathematics for Finance and Data (formerly DEA Lamberton), jointly offered by Université Gustave Eiffel and Université Paris-Est Créteil, and co-accredited with École nationale des ponts et chaussées, obtained in 2022. This program included an internship at Next Gate Tech, a fintech company based in Luxembourg, where I worked as a quantitative developer. I also hold a master’s degree in artificial intelligence from ENSTA, obtained in 2021, during which I completed an internship at Generali France where I worked as a data scientist.
