About

PhD Candidate in Applied Mathematics at Laboratoire des Signaux et Systèmes (L2S), Université Paris-Saclay, CNRS, CentraleSupélec

I conduct my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Abdel Lisser, Université Paris-Saclay and Prof. Sihem Tebbani, CentraleSupélec. My research interests lie in probability, optimisation, optimal control, optimal transport, machine learning, and financial mathematics.

Curriculum

Before starting my doctoral contract at Université Paris-Saclay, I worked as a quantitative analyst contractor at BNP Paribas. I worked in a quantitative research team on equity derivatives.

I graduated from the master's degree Mathematics for Finance and Data at Université Gustave Eiffel & Université Paris-Est Créteil (ex-DEA Lamberton) in 2022. I completed this degree with an internship at Next Gate Tech, a FinTech company based in Luxembourg, working as a quantitative developer.

I graduated from the master's degree from ENSTA in artificial intelligence in 2021. I completed this degree with an internship at Generali France working as a data scientist.

I am 27 years old. I have French nationality and I am also a Spanish citizen.
